Commonly asked questions

How many pokemons are on site?

- 905 including generation 8.

Can I add more than 1 pokemon to favourites?

- Unfortunately you can't. By introducing this feature I wanted users to not be able to like more than 1 pokemon, because then the ranking of the most liked pokemons would not be reliable.

What features are you planning to implement at this moment?

- Admin subpage that will handle some high level operations on pokemons (e.g. adding new)

- More pokemon stats and interactions between them

- Website responsiveness for mobiles and custom resolutions
